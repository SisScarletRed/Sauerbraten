Sauerbraten Demo Files

Those are files that record all the server-events of a game..

Features

This client features:

  • /demostart: Start recording
  • /demostop: Stop recording
  • /demoauto: Autorecord demos
  • Demo previewing (as shown in the image)
  • Improved serverdemo-downloading userfriendliness (aka: better gui)

The demos will be saved to home/$demosdir. That's where the Client searches for demos for previewing them, though demos from all over the PC can be played

Compatibility

Demos recorded with this client cannot be played with other clients (though I might write a converter for that).
That is, because the so called "Magic" and the Version has changed. The client can still play other demos recorded serverside. But the clientside-recorded demos include more information, such as when and who recorded it (you can set your name with /recordername *name*) and IPs of the players, if available.
