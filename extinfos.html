Extinfos

Get infos about the players and servers without having to connect to one!

Features

This client features:

  • Serverpreviews: A scoreboard of the players on that server.. even when you're not connected
  • Playerbrowser: A list of all players that connected the the masterserver
  • WhoIs: A Name-IP database, where you can e.g. check what other names a player has used
  • Ipignore: Don't only ignore by clientnum, but also by IP!
